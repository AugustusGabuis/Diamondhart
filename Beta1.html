Diamondhart Hazmat Company

Alpha1

Containment Difficulty: 0

Danger Level: 3

Benefit Level: 3

Description:

Beta1 is a building, inside the building is a satanic star, at the center of the star is a piece of wood with candles and an animal skull on it.

When the candles are lit, a small earthquake will happen within 300 feet, the earthquake has been classified as 3.4 on the Richter Scale.

During the 3.4 Scale earthquake, a portal will begin to open on the ground, What happens next is depending on what skull is on the piece of wood.

Deer Skull - A dark red portal opens, inside is a cavern with red rock and humanoid beings with red skin, wings, and horns. Portal known as Beta1-A1, Humanoids inside known as Beta1-A2.

Human Skull - A sky blue portal opens, inside a 18th century European village is visible, in the distance a bigger city is visible. Portal known as Beta1-B1.

Rat Skull - A dirt brown portal opens, inside is a sewer, there seems to be some kind of monster inside of the sewer. Portal known as Beta1-C1

Jaguar Skull - A light green portal opens, inside is a jungle, there are hundreds of big cats surrounding a village. Portal known as Beta1-D1, cats known as Beta1-D2

Cat Skull - A light gray portal opens, inside is a alleyway, the alleyway is endless presumably, and there are hostile cats that grow in size when threatened. Portal known as Beta1-E1, Cats known as Beta1-E2

Dog Skull - A green portal opens, inside a large plains area is visible, dog barking can be heard in distance. Portal known as Beta1-F1

Fossil Skull - A black w/ accents of rainbow portal opens, inside is a room, a grandfather clock the size of a skyscraper is visible. Inside is a keyboard with buttons, When you press one a screen appears, and a video plays, it seems to be a life of a dinosaur first person. Portal known as Beta1-G1, Clock known as Beta1-G2.

Alpha1-B Skull - A mirror world appears, ghost versions of people can be seen, these ghosts arent interactable, but when you touch them, they do feel it. Communication doesnt happen with these people. Ghosts of dead people do appear, those versions can be communicated with. Portal known as Beta1-H1, Non-interactable ghosts known as Beta1-H2, Interactable ghosts known as Beta1-H3.

Beta1-A2 Skull - A Portal that is pure white opens, inside is a large forest, everything appears to be made of human organs/flesh, including trees. There are large squid-like creatures that appear to be parrasites of the trees, using their tenticles to absorb their blood sap. Portal known as Beta1-I1, Trees known as Beta1-I2, Creatures known as Beta1-I3.

Uses:

Excellent Method of fleeing a place, I mean potentially hundreds of dimensions to go, no one would find us.

Beta1-H1 can be used as a way to spy.

Certain Dimensions may have more advanced technology.
