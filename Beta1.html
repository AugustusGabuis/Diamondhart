Containment Difficulty: 0

Danger Level: 3

Benefit Level: 3

Description:

Beta1 is a building, inside the building is a satanic star, at the center of the star is a piece of wood with candles and an animal skull on it.

When the candles are lit, a small earthquake will happen within 300 feet, the earthquake has been classified as 3.4 on the Richter Scale.

During the 3.4 Scale earthquake, a portal will begin to open on the ground, Lucas Bjorn and Dianna Gialen have thought this portal was to Hell.

They thought that because all you can see is a deep red, similar to the cultist circle.

Inside the portal is a large cavern, humanoid beings can be seen, Hereby known as Beta1-A. Beta1-A instances usually have red skin and wings

It is unknown if Beta1-A is hostile, Beta1-A could potentially even be sentient, if so, there is a whole other civilization under that portal.

If there is, it is neccisary that we contact this civilization.

Benefits:

It's literally hell, the benefits of having a portal to hell are endless.

If we ally the potential civilization, we can get more military.

We could also mine the minerals of Hell.
