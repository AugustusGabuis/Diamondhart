Diamondhart Anomaly Classifications

At the top of files, you may have seen different classes

Let's see what they are.

Containment Difficulty is how difficult it is to contain a specific anomaly.

0 - No effort to contain

1 - Some effort to contain

2 - Takes effort to contain

3 - Substantial Effort to contain

4 - Large effort to contain

5 - Impossible to contain


Danger Level is how dangerous it is

0 - Harms no one

1 - Potential Danger

2 - Apparent Danger

3 - Danger

4 - World Ending

5 - Universal End


Finally, Benefit Level.

0 - Not Benefitial

1 - Financially Benefitial

2 - Logistically Benefitial

3 - Benefitial to other anomalies containment

4 - Militarily benefitial

5 - All of the above

And thats all of the Diamondhart Anomaly Classes.
