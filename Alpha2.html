Descriptions:

Alpha2 is a saucer-shapped building, nothing more could be known until the National Guard allowed Diamondhart to study it.

Alpha2's inside is split into 3 rings, the first ring has a lot of lights, it is unknown what these lights do, it could be core parts

Alpha2's inner most rings have human chambers, some with humans, most without, they are probably dead.

Alpha2's inner most ring has a core, and when things enter the core, they begin to defy gravity
