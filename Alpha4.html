Diamondhart Hazmat Company

Alpha4

Containment Difficulty: 0

Danger Level: 1

Benefit Level: 4

Description:

Alpha4 is a vending machine that may be sentient.

When someone psychically harms Alpha4, the person will be teleported inside the vending machine.

Alpha4 seems to have a strange sense of karma, the more harm that comes to Alpha4, the longer the person will be imprisoned inside of it.

Alpha4 does instantly regenerate when damaged.

Benefits:

Eternal prison if you get someone to explode Alpha4.
