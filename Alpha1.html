Alpha1

Containment Difficulty: 0

Danger Level: 2

Benefit Level: 2

Description:

Alpha1 is a seemingly normal arcade machine, until you play it, when you play Alpha1, you get teleported into Alpha1-P, a pocket dimension.

There are three rooms in Alpha1-P, The room you teleport into is a white room, with a door you can get through thats also white.

Next is a hallway, where there are two doors, one leads to what looks like a room to watch the first room.

Finally, the other door lets you leave Alpha1-P and return to where the arcade machine is.

Alpha1 has some interesting properties, the first being it's regeneration. When Alpha1-A is damaged, slowly the damage will regenerate.

It is unknown what happens when someone is inside while it gets damaged.

When Alpha1 gets unplugged, the people in Alpha1-A begin seeing creatures in Alpha1-A, aswell as they begin getting intense migranes.

After 5 minutes in Alpha1-A, the walls of Alpha1-A will collaspe, and the person inside will find themselves in a black void.

After 10 minutes, any creatures will turn hostile, trying to kill the subject.

Usually, the creatures will manage to kill the subject, but if the subject takes any illegal substance, it'll become as if they just got into Alpha1.

After, The subject will see these creatures for presumably ever.

The creatures in Alpha1-A (Creatures are Alpha1-B) have 2 states, passive state is where Alpha1-B isn't solid and is passive.

The hostile state of Alpha1-B is solid, and well hostile.

Benefits:

Excellent torture method.

Excellent storage facility, as objects don't get damaged.
