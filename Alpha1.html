Alpha1

Containment Difficulty: 0
Danger Level: 2
Benefit Level: 3
Description:
Alpha1 is an arcade machine that was in [CLASSIFIED], it is in possession of the Diamondhart Foundation.
When played, the player is teleported into an unknown area, hereby called 'Alpha1-P'
Radio signals don't work in Alpha1-P, suggesting its in a pocket dimension.
Alpha1-P consists of 3 rooms, one is a empty room with white walls and floors, this room has a hidden door that leads to the next room
The next room is a hallway, with 2 doors. One leads to a different room, whitch appears to be a studio, One of the walls is revealed to be a invisible glass
The Other door is an escape back to the arcade.
Alpha1 can be played when holding an item, making Alpha1 a potential storage facility.
When Alpha1 is unplugged and a biological being is in Alpha1-P, after 2 minutes Alpha1-P will begin getting distorted, while Alpha1 is unplugged, it is impossible to escape.
The Subject will begin experiencing both mental and psychical pain.
One subject said they saw a creature.
