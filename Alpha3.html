Diamondhart Hazmat Company

Alpha3

Containment Difficulty: 1

Danger Level: 2

Benefit Level: 4

Description:

Alpha3 is a barrel drum that is always on fire.

Alpha3's fire can melt through anything.

Wood melts in only 5 seconds.

Metal melts in only 2 minutes.

Due to metal being so easily melted, We believe that anything can be melted using Alpha3.

In theory, using an element that has a reaction to heat could cause some dangerous reactions.

Benefits:

Easy disposal of corpses and other things

Potential way to destroy other anomalies
